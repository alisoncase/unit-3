Beyond the Skyline: Chicago's Unequal Opportunity Landscape

Alison Case | November 2024

Chicago, a city renowned for its diversity and vitality, is also marked by significant social and economic disparities. Over the past decades, the gap between the city's wealthiest and poorest neighborhoods has widened, leading to significant disparities in health, education, and economic opportunity. This map, powered by data from the Chicago Health Atlas, visualizes the stark differences in 11 key indicators across Chicago's 77 community areas. From income inequality to health outcomes, the map unveils a city divided, where wealth and opportunity are unevenly distributed.
